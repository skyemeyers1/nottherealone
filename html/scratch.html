Growing up with Utah's Wasatch Front as my backyard instilled in me a deep passion for freeride skiing. Surrounded by towering peaks and pristine powder, I was inspired from a young age to explore the mountains beyond the groomed trails. Freeride skiing is a dynamic and thrilling discipline that combines big mountain terrain with the creativity of freestyle. Skiers navigate natural, off-piste landscapes, tackling cliffs, steep chutes, and untouched snow with precision and flair. It’s a sport that demands technical skill, adaptability, and a strong connection to the environment—values I’ve embraced throughout my life in the mountains.
